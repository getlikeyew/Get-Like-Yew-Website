What We Do

Get Like Yew is a brand new health & wellness platform. We aim to meet people where they are in life and provide them the with necessary tools and information to be the best version of themselves. Why? Because it's the only person we get to be! Why aim to be someone else when there's so many of ways you can "Get Like Yew". Whatever your interest - there's probably something here for you! If you're new to the site, here's a quick breakdown:

  • Exercise - Over 75+ exercises arranged by muscle group, basic training/equipment know-how with supporting diagrams.
  • Nutrition - A comprehensive breakdown of macro and micronutrients, food & dietary information & more useful charts.
  • Developers - Check us out on GitHub! This website started as a coding project and your feedback can help its improvement. I'll also be detailing my journey into becoming a developer including books, platforms, additional resources and a host of other fun topics - like all the cool APIs used on this website: Spotify, GoogleMaps, etc.

In the Works...

  • Video Content - We're currently creating a self-guided Beginner Workout Program which will be available for purchase in the coming months. Check us out on Youtube and Instagram!.
  • Hair & Skin Care - Coming soon! Exercise and nutrition are our bread 'n butter but as we look to expand our offering we see these topics as a natural progression. We will be looking for content creators. Interested? Please contact us! - Send Email

Who Am I?

I've always been an active person since playing tag & basketball in the neighborhood to football and track in middleschool and high school. I consider Costa Rica my second home where I attended United World College - sad to say I'm still basura en futbol but i did pick up an appreciation for ultimate frisbee.

How Did I Get Here?

I came to Columbia University to study Financial Economics. I was still exercising but it became boring without a competitive goal. That feeling led me to join the taekowndo team for a few years before deciding to build my repertoire as a health professional on the side. I have team and individual sports to thank for inspiring me to combine my interest in teaching and fitness.
