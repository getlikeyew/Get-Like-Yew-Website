Programs About

Physcial activity is non-negotiable! Humans were made to move, plain and simple. The baseline recommendation is at least 150 minutes a week of moderate-intensity physical activity. There are countless benefits to exercise and many risks that come with its absence. Of course, the gym is not the only way to be physcially active. There are tons of other things to do across sports, leisure, daily activities etc. This section focuses on the basics of muscle and cardio training.

  • Improved cardiorespiratory and muscular fitness
  • Improved bone health
  • Lower risk of coronary heart disease
  • lower risk of stroke
  • Reduced symptons of depression
  • Better cognitive function
  • Improved sleep quality
